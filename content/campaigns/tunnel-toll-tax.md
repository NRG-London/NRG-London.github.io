## The story so far

Freedom of Information requests have revealed that the Blackwall and Silvertown tunnel charging scheme is generating more income from fines than from tolls — raising serious questions about whether this is a congestion management tool or a revenue trap.

## Key findings

The data shows a scheme where more money is being extracted through penalties than through the published charges. This is not what Londoners were told would happen when the Silvertown tunnel was approved.

## What I'm doing about it

I've submitted a detailed comment to The Times setting out the FOI findings and calling for full transparency from TfL on the scheme's finances. I'm also raising this directly with the Mayor through the Assembly's scrutiny process.
