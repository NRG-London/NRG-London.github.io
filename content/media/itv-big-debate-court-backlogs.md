Appeared on ITV London's The Big Debate to discuss the growing crisis in court backlogs and its impact on criminal justice in London. This was the first time a London Assembly Member had appeared on the programme.

The appearance generated cross-party engagement, including a public endorsement from a Labour MP on the court backlogs issue.
